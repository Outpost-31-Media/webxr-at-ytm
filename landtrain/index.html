YTM VIRTUAL MUSEUM

LeTourneau LCC-1 Sno-Train.


This model is at true to life scale so go find a large open area to view it in.

Once you start the experience look around with your phone to let it get a feel of your suroundings, once it detects the ground a grid will appear to help you place the Land Train where you want, once you are happy with the placement tap your screen and the model will appear.
